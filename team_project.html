Introduction

Background: Adolescents live in a sensitive phase that requires significant psychological and social support. Psychological and nervous pressures increase among young people aged 15 to 20 due to hormonal changes, academic stress, and family expectations.

Importance of the Topic: The mental health of adolescents is one of the key factors for their future success and happiness. Providing early support can improve the quality of life and prevent the worsening of psychological issues.

Organization's Vision

Goal: To provide psychological support and assistance to adolescents experiencing psychological and nervous pressures, including academic stress, family expectations, and social problems in relationships.

Mission: To create a supportive and safe environment for adolescents to assess their psychological problems and receive appropriate support for free.

How the Organization Works

Developing a Mobile Application

  • Psychological Tests: Conduct tests to assess the psychological state of adolescents, identify pressures, and mental health issues.
  • Providing Solutions: Offer tips, practical guidance, educational resources, and specialized consultations.

Support and Assistance

  • Free Consultations: Provide counseling sessions and connect adolescents with mental health specialists.
  • Additional Resources: Provide a resource library, organize workshops, and offer study schedule organization tools.

Awareness for Parents

  • Awareness Campaigns: Organize campaigns and training seminars for parents.

Funding

Donation Model: The organization relies on charitable donations. A fundraising campaign targets individuals, companies, and institutions.

Transparency: Periodic reports ensure that funds are used effectively.

Expected Impact

  • On Adolescents: Improve mental health, enhance coping abilities, and build positive relationships.
  • On Parents: Increase awareness and understanding between parents and adolescents.
  • On Society: Raise mental health awareness and provide a replicable model.

Implementation Steps

  • Phase One: Develop the application, prepare materials, and organize seminars.
  • Phase Two: Launch the awareness campaign, provide services, and hold seminars.
  • Phase Three: Evaluate the program and improve tools based on feedback.
